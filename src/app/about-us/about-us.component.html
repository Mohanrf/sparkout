A Brief History of Sparkout

Great ideas are born in the mind and crafted with passionate hands.

Our journey began in 2017 but the vision was embedded in our founders’ minds way before that. From our humble beginnings as only a 6-member startup, we’re today one of the leading software development businesses, delivering innovative solutions to global customers and pioneering in Blockchain Technology.

Our Vision

To explore the cosmos of technological innovation and create opportunities that impact human lives.

Our Mission

What we believe in

Our Core Values

We believe in the power of human thought and passion. Our culture and work ethic reflect the same.

Client Satisfaction

The reason our customers prefer us is because we understand the value of human relationships. We don’t consider ourselves as just a service provider but a support system that won’t be happy unless everything is just right for our clients.

User-Centric Approach

Our Journey

A timeline of Sparkout from the Spark to the light

2023

Codespot Foundation

We've always tried to impart our knowledge to students with informative talks and workshops. 2023 marks a huge milestone for us as we established the Codespot Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to helping students of all walks of life find their career paths, learn new skills, and interact with industry leaders.

2022

New Office Opening

The year started off with hope, as we opened our new office, "House of Innovation." The occasion was graced by Thiru Mano Thangaraj, the Honourable Minister of Information and Technology, Government of Tamil Nadu. We expanded our development capabilities and our team is now bigger than ever.

2021

Building a family

We were now bit by the blockchain bug, working with innovative startups on both blockchain and non-blockchain solutions. Now, we were a strong family of 70+ passionate individuals, each one just as passionate as the next. We expanded our reach to multiple countries including the Middle-East.

2020

Creating Experiences

Even as the pandemic hit, it was business as usual for all of our teammates. We expanded our family to 60+, with nearly 150+ projects behind us and a loyal global clientele. We started working on complex and interesting projects for both blockchain and non-blockchain use cases.

2019

New Horizons

2019 was a monumental year for us as we ventured into exploring Blockchain Technology and attended our very first Blockchain conference at Thailand, now with a team size of 35+. We founded our Blockchain subsidiary, BlockchainX, which is a sister concern completely dedicated to building blockchain solutions.

2018

Paving the way forward

With just a year behind us and many more to follow, we expanded our team size to 25+ and were already working on applications for multiple brands. Getting started with real-world projects early-on helped us establish our name as a leading global software development startup.

2017

The Genesis

Sparkout Tech’s team of 6 set out on the journey to change people's lives through innovative solutions. Our team loves to build things that solve real-world problems and this collective ambition is what drove the original team to take the leap into the world of software technology.
