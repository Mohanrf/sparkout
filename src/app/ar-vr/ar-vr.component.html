Gaming

Build immersive games and apps

Create highly immersive games that blur the line between reality and cyberspace. This technology can redefine how we experience gaming with things such as the Metaverse which has unlimited possibilities. Build a game that your users can never get enough of and design virtual economies where people can live, trade, and grow.

Retail

Access products without touch

E-commerce is one of the most booming industries and makes billions a year. You can create a platform to sell your products where your users can use AR/VR to visualize what they are buying without ever touching the product. This is beneficial for products such as furniture, eyewear, apparel, and much more.

Healthcare

AR/VR-aided surgeries & training

Allow patients to access world-class surgeons and experts even if they are halfway across the world. With robotics technology, surgeons can perform surgeries using specialized tools and VR headsets. You can also use AR to visualize the human anatomy and plan treatments in a non-invasive manner, seamlessly.

Real Estate

Visualize construction and design

AR/VR in real estate can dramatically cut down your costs and the time that you usually take in design. We can build applications for you that can visualize 3-Dimensional models of buildings, houses, and much more, directly through the camera of your smartphone. This is especially helpful for architects and businesses.

Deliveries

VR-assisted drone deliveries

VR technology allows you to make deliveries to remote locations without ever having to directly go to these locations. You can use long-range drones coupled with Virtual Reality headsets to remotely deliver anything from groceries to pharmaceutical products to places that are otherwise less accessible.

Aviation

Train pilots before they take flight

Airplanes carry hundreds of people at a time and gallons of fuel that can be disastrous if anything goes wrong. Pilots go through extensive training to fly these amazing machines. Not only can you train pilots using Virtual Reality technology, but you can also visualize possible disasters and flight trajectories with real-world data.

Automotive

Embark on unmanned missions

There are some parts of the world where it's too dangerous physically be present. Using VR technology, you can easily send remotely-operated vehicles to deliver goods. You can also use this technology to conduct rescue operations with unmanned vehicles using VR headsets. This is not only safer but also cuts down manpower costs.
