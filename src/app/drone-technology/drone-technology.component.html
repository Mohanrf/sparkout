Agriculture

The industry can benefit greatly when it comes to using Drone technology. UAVs can cover large agricultural land for use cases such as crop monitoring, surveillance, and even insurance surveys. It's not always easy to process insurance claims related to crop damage. Well, now it is.

Transportation

The major component of efficient transportation, no matter in which segment, is infrastructure. Using Aerial mapping, engineers can easily survey, monitor, and measure larger areas and plan construction accordingly. All this can be done while sitting in one place and without spending several hours.

Renewable energy

One of the major challenges in the renewable energy sector is maintenance. Some of these tasks such as maintenance of turbine blades require a worker actually putting themselves in dangerous situations. With Drones, this can all be done with a remote control.

Mining

The major benefit that you get with Drones or UAVs is accessibility. Mining has always had workplace hazards and people are forced to work in these hazardous conditions. Drones allow your workers to survey and monitor hazardous areas without putting themselves at risk.

Oil and Gas

Oil and Gas mining rigs are some of the most dangerous places to work not only because of the danger of harmful substances and fumes but also because of the volatility of the substances. Drones help your workforce access these dangerous areas safely.

Search & Rescue

Search and rescue operations are perhaps the areas where we have all seen Drones at work. You can access otherwise impossible-to-get-to areas using Drones and plan search & rescue operations without putting the personnel's lives at risk.
