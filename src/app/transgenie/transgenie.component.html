What we do

Solution under Transgenie

TransGenie is a technology solution that we've developed to help businesses manage their supply chain, fleet, and field operations. Our approach has been to make TransGenie a modular technology with multiple avenues such as AI, Blockchain, Automation, etc., to deliver a customized product that makes your business frictionless.

Supplier Relationship Management(SRM)

Our SRM software accurately tracks orders and helps your business streamline supplier selection, onboarding, performance tracking, validation, and document management.

Blockchain-Based Supply Chain Tracking

We leverage blockchain technology to provide transparent, secure & traceable supply chain tracking. It lets businesses track & verify the movement of goods, ensuring authenticity, reducing the risk of counterfeit products, and so on.

Internet of Things(IOT)

Our blockchain-powered IoT network enables secure, frictionless communication between sensors, distributed ledgers, and databases to optimize supply chain visibility and quality assurance.

Industry Process Management

Our IPM solution optimizes & automates various processes within the supply chain. It contains features like workflow management, document management, task automation, improving efficiency & enhancing overall productivity.

Order Management

Our order management solution provides end-to-end visibility & control over the order fulfillment process with features like order capture, inventory management, order tracking, customer communication, and ensuring timely orders.

Lean Management

Not just the software we build, but our entire team will support your project continuously throughout the process and beyond, like lean management.

Fleet Management

We enable businesses to efficiently manage their fleets of vehicles in logistics & transportation. It includes features like route optimization, vehicle tracking, fuel management, driver performance monitoring, & so on.

AR-Powered Warehouse Automation

We leverage AR technology to enhance warehouse operations with features like AR-guided picking, inventory management, warehouse layout optimization & improving accuracy, speed & efficiency in warehouse processes.

Delivery Dispatch

Our delivery dispatch solution revolutionizes the coordination and management of delivery operations by providing features like seamless scheduling, real-time tracking, route optimization, proof of delivery & timely deliveries while enhancing customer satisfaction.
