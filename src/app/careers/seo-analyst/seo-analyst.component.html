Digital Marketing Executive

Job Description

As a Digital Marketing Specialist, you will be responsible for developing, implementing and managing marketing campaigns that promote our company and its products or services. You will play a major role in enhancing brand awareness within the digital space as well as driving website traffic and acquiring leads/customers. You will work closely with cross-functional teams, including creative, content, development and sales to create effective campaigns that achieve our marketing objectives.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement digital marketing campaigns across multiple platforms, including social media, email, display advertising and search engine marketing.
  • Monitor and analyze the effectiveness of campaigns, using metrics such as click-through rate, conversion rate and ROI, and adjust strategies as needed to optimize results.
  • Create and manage engaging content for social media platforms, email campaigns and other digital channels.
  • Collaborate with the creative team to develop visual and multimedia content that aligns with brand messaging and objectives.
  • Coordinate with the content team to ensure website content is optimized for search engines (SEO) and aligned with overall marketing messaging.
  • Manage and grow the company's email database, creating targeted email campaigns that drive engagement and conversions.
  • Identify new digital marketing opportunities and stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies.
  • Work closely with sales teams to support lead generation efforts and improve lead-to-customer conversion rates.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure that digital campaigns are aligned with overall marketing objectives and brand messaging.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in marketing, communications, or a related field
  • At least 3 years of experience in digital marketing.
  • Experience with email marketing, social media marketing, search engine marketing, and display advertising.
  • Knowledge of SEO, Google Analytics and other web analytics tools.
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Strong project management skills, with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Ability to think creatively and strategically to develop effective marketing campaigns.
  • Knowledge of HTML/CSS and experience with CMS platforms is a plus.

If you are a creative thinker with a passion for digital marketing and the ability to work collaboratively to achieve results, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity!
