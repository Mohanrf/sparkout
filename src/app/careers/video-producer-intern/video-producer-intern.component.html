Video Production Team Intern

Overview

Qualification: Visual Communication

Level: Freshers

Job Description:

We are looking for a highly motivated and creative individual to join our Video Production Team as an intern. The successful candidate will assist in the production of high-quality video content for a variety of platforms, not limited to, social media, websites, and events.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in pre-production tasks such as researching, writing, and creating storyboards for video projects
  • Support the Video Production Team in setting up and breaking down equipment for shoots
  • Participate in video shoots and provide support as needed
  • Contribute to post-production tasks including editing, color correction, and audio mixing
  • Learn and utilize video editing software and equipment

Requirements:

  • Pursuing a degree in Film, Video Production, Communication, or a related field
  • Strong organizational and project management skills
  • Ability to multitask and prioritize in a fast-paced environment
  • Familiarity with video production equipment and software, not limited to, camera, lighting, and editing software
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • A passion for video production and a strong desire to learn and grow in the field
