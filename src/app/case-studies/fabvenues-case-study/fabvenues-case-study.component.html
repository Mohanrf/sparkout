About

Fabvenues - Your One-Stop Venue
Rental Marketplace!

Fabvenues is an online venue rental marketplace and platform for booking portals for venues. It serves as a single platform where individuals and businesses can find, compare, and book venues for various purposes such as stays or events. Fabvenues aims to simplify the venue booking process by offering a diverse range of venues from across India, providing convenience and choice to users. Whether you are looking for a farmhouse, a marriage hall, or a space in a mall for business promotion, Fabvenues is the single destination where your search ends! Fabvenues helps connect users with their desired venues, making it easier to find the perfect space for their needs.

Fabvenues caters to hosts and travellers seeking venue rentals, and it serves as a convenient and efficient platform for hosts to showcase their venues and for travellers to discover, book, and enjoy a variety of venues.

HOW IT WORKS - ADMINS

Admin Dashboard 1

Fabvenues provides admins with a dedicated dashboard that serves as a centralized control panel. From here, admins can manage various aspects of the platform, including user accounts, venue listings, bookings, and overall platform settings.

User Management 2

Admins have the ability to manage user accounts on Fabvenues. This includes tasks such as user registration, user verification, and account moderation. Admins may also handle user inquiries, feedback, and support requests.

Venue Listings 3

Admins oversee the process of adding and managing venue listings on the platform. They ensure that each listing meets the platform's quality standards and guidelines. This involves reviewing and approving venue submissions, verifying details, and ensuring accurate and up-to-date information is displayed.

Booking Management 4

Admins are responsible for managing the booking process. They monitor and review booking requests made by users, ensuring the availability and accuracy of information. Admins also handle any modifications, cancellations, or disputes that may arise during the booking process.

Communication and Notifications 5

Admins facilitate communication between users and venue owners. They may handle inquiries, assist with negotiation processes, and resolve conflicts if necessary. Admins also ensure that users receive timely notifications and updates regarding their bookings, payments, and any changes to their reservations.

Platform Maintenance and Upgrades 6

Admins oversee the maintenance and improvement of the Fabvenues platform. This includes monitoring system performance, resolving technical issues, and implementing software updates and security patches. Admins continuously evaluate user feedback and industry trends to identify areas for improvement and implement new features or enhancements to enhance the user experience.

Compliance and Policies 7

Admins ensure that Fabvenues adheres to relevant laws, regulations, and policies. They monitor compliance with terms of service, privacy policies, and other legal requirements. Admins may also handle content moderation, ensuring that listings and user-generated content on the platform comply with community guidelines and standards.

HOW IT WORKS - HOSTS

Listing 1

The property owners, managers, and hosts list their property with all the details that they would like their travellers to know about it. Travellers get to see Property photos, property details, offerings like flowers & decor, snacks, food, and drinks (options), Room types, and tariffs that they can choose from and information like payment terms, cancellation policy, check-in and check-out times, and other relevant details (All this information comes from the property listing by the host).

Booking 2

Travellers can connect directly with the host for inquiries about a property, price, booking dates, etc. using the 'Chat with Host' option. The Traveller can book the place directly by choosing dates (if the option chosen by the host is 'Instant Booking'), or the Traveller can request to book. The host can release an invoice for the stay and/or offerings to the traveller for making payment to book the place.

Payment from Traveller 3

Once the first part (in the case of a 2/3 part payment) is paid, the booking is confirmed, and the calendar automatically gets blocked for that date. The payment made by the traveller is held in a virtual account, and it gets transferred to the host account (the bank account that's updated on Fabvenues profile) (as per the policy that can be checked at the time of listing).

Reservation & calendar 4

Admins are responsible for managing the booking process. They monitor and review booking requests made by users, ensuring the availability and accuracy of information. Admins also handle any modifications, cancellations, or disputes that may arise during the booking process.

Welcome the Traveller 5

You are all set to welcome the traveller to your place.

Cancellation 6

In case of cancellation of the reservation either by the traveller or the host, the respective cancellation policy holds.

HOW IT WORKS - TRAVELLER

Search and Discovery 1

Travellers begin by visiting the Fabvenues website or app and entering their event details, such as location, date, and event type. The platform then presents a curated list of venues that match their criteria.

Advanced Filtering 2

Fabvenues provides advanced search filters that allow travellers to refine their search based on specific preferences. They can filter by capacity, amenities, price range, and more, ensuring they find venues that meet their specific requirements.

Venue Profiles and Information 3

Travellers can explore detailed venue profiles that provide comprehensive information about each listing. This includes photos, descriptions, available amenities, capacity, pricing details, and any additional features or services offered by the venue.

Reviews and Ratings 4

Travellers can read reviews and ratings from previous guests who have booked and stayed at the venues. These reviews provide valuable insights and help travellers make informed decisions based on the experiences of others.

Communication with Hosts 5

Travellers can directly communicate with venue hosts through the Fabvenues platform. They can ask questions, discuss specific requirements, negotiate terms, and seek clarifications before making a booking decision.

Booking and Payment 6

Once travellers have selected their preferred venue, they can proceed with the booking process. Fabvenues offers a secure payment system that allows travellers to make the payment online, ensuring a safe and convenient transaction.

Confirmation and Itinerary 7

After the booking is confirmed and payment is processed, travellers receive a booking confirmation along with an itinerary that includes details such as venue address, check-in and check-out times, and any other relevant information provided by the host.

Support and Assistance 8

Fabvenues provides customer support to assist travellers throughout their booking journey. If travellers have any questions or encounter any issues, they can reach out to the Fabvenues support team for prompt assistance and resolution.

Event/Stay Experience 9

Travellers have the opportunity to enjoy their event at the booked venue. Whether it's a conference, meeting, or any other gathering, they can make use of the venue's facilities and amenities to create a successful and memorable event.
