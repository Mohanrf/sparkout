Working Model Of FirstBlastIt

FirstBlast consists of segmented working conditions based on the user's and publisher's subscriptions plan. In general, the application allows a single user to one only access the first 200 audio files under any subcategory and the cycle goes on updating in consecutive numbers moving the older files to stage two. The current playlist will only have 200 however. There are different sub-categories under each category giving a vast and in-depth knowledge of the same.

As a new user, after registering in the application OPT is sent to the email and phone to verify. Once the verification is validated, the user gets access to the platform. Any user logged in for the first time will enjoy the privilege of the Free Subscription module, that further can be updated and downgraded at any point of time.

To upload an audio file, the user has to check on these.
  • The audio file should be a minimum of 10 secs.
  • Maximum 2 minutes and not more

The audio can be either recorded in the platform directly or uploaded from Internal File storages/ Google Drive / Onedrive/ Dropbox. After choosing the file, Click on Next and enter the details like Title, choose Category, and subcategory. Then fill in the SPC (Self Promotional Content) details ( not mandatory). To add on, there are options to also download the audio to the Listener’s local drive.

When it's filed, the listener with the help of Get SPC button can derive the details of the publisher when enabled.

Similarly, there is a BTM (Bring back To Me) button that gives the publishers the details of listeners of the audio.

Coming forward,

There are Four Track Modules for the audio to slide through in the application.

Waiting Track 1

- any audio recorded or uploaded stay here, before getting to the next stage. Once verified and validated the audio then goes to the next track for listening.

Current Track 2

- only 200 audios are played from each subcategory. The queue is well managed by the admin team and it is processed as per the First in First out Concept.

Retro Track 3

- when the 201st track adds in the current track, The very 1st audio there comes to the Retro tracks. The cycle is also maintained here in order to maintain updates.

Deleted Track 4

- the first track from the Retro comes in here as that is lined with 200 tracks under the subcategory. This sticks to the idea that the oldest comes out first.

The audio in the deleted track will be visible to the punisher in the uploaded file while it won't be visible to the listener. It can be reuploaded by the publisher.

Moreover, the count of 200 can be increased or decreased in the future by the admin based on requirements.

As mentioned earlier the privileges of the user in FirstBlastIt are based on their subscription plans, there 4 module to choose from.

Free membership

The listeners can access any number of videos but only be able to publish 3 audio per month. With 3 clicks to access either SPC or BTM.

Standard membership

Similarly upload 3 audio per month. SPC and BTM have unlimited access for the users here.

Premium membership

Everything is unlimited for the users here. The number of audio published, SPC, and BTM are free to access.

Premium Plus membership

Everything is unlimited for the users here. The number of audio published, SPC, and BTM are free to access. Additionally, users get the option to rotate their audio 3 times in the cycle.

In order to manage bulk data processing fast and securely in the background, we have brought in Queue functions with Laravel to achieve it.

Renewal process

The users can opt for Monthly or Yearly packages with auto-renewal options. The first card uploaded in the Credit Card Management section will stay as Default, it cannot be deleted while others can be. Any number of cards can be added and chosen as default. Meanwhile, the subscription can be upgraded or downgraded.
